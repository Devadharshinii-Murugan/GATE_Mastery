GATE Syllabus 2025 - General Aptitude

The GATE Syllabus 2025 for General Aptitude is common for all papers. It holds a total of 15% weightage in the GATE exam.Check out the topic-wise GATE Syllabus 2025 for General Aptitude in the table below:

Sl. No. Syllabus
1. Verbal Ability
2. Quantitative Aptitude
3. Analytical Aptitude

Verbal Aptitude

Topic Description
Tenses Verbal tense usage
Adjectives Understanding and usage of adjectives
Articles Usage of articles in sentences
Words and Phrases Understanding and usage of words and phrases
Vocabulary Building and understanding vocabulary
Parts of speech Understanding different parts of speech
Idioms Usage and understanding of idiomatic expressions
Prepositions Usage of prepositions in sentences
Conjunctions Understanding and usage of conjunctions
Verb-noun agreement Agreement of verbs with their subjects
Comprehension & reading Understanding and analyzing written passages
Narrative sequencing Sequencing of events in a narrative
Sentence completion Completing sentences with appropriate words
Verbal analogies Understanding relationships between words
Critical reasoning Understanding and analyzing arguments
Word groups Grouping of words based on similarities

Analytical Aptitude

Topic Description
Logic: deduction and induction Logical deduction and induction
Analogy Analogical reasoning
Number relations & reasoning Relationships between numbers and logical reasoning
Coding-Decoding Decoding patterns and codes
Decision Making Ability to make decisions based on given conditions
Direction Sense Understanding directions and spatial relationships
Blood Relation Understanding familial relationships
Data Sufficiency Assessing sufficiency of given data to answer questions

Spatial Aptitude

Topic Description
Mirroring Understanding shape mirroring
Assembling Understanding shape assembling
Rotation Understanding shape rotation
Scaling Understanding scale transformation
Translation Understanding shape translation
Paper folding & 2-D and 3-D patterns Understanding paper folding and 2-D/3-D patterns
Grouping Grouping similar shapes or objects
Papercutting Understanding shape cutting and rearranging

Numerical Aptitude

Topic Description
Elementary Statistics & probability Understanding basic statistics and probability concepts
Geometry Understanding geometric shapes and concepts
Data interpretation Interpreting data presented in various formats
Data- Graphs (bar graph, histogram, pie chart, and other data graphs), 2- and 3- dimensional plots, Maps, and Tables Understanding and interpreting data presented in graphical and tabular forms
Mensuration Calculating measurements of geometric shapes
Numerical computation & estimation- Powers, Series, Exponents, Percentages, Permutations, Combinations, Ratios, and Logarithms Performing numerical computations and estimations
Numerical reasoning Reasoning with numerical data
