What is GATE?

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level examination conducted to assess the understanding of various engineering and science subjects among undergraduate students and graduates. It serves as a gateway for admission to postgraduate programs in prestigious institutions and for recruitment in public sector companies. GATE scores are also used for fellowship programs offered by government agencies and for opportunities in research and development.

GATE exam is for

With the GATE exam score, you can get admission to Master of Engineering (M.E.)/Master of Technology (M.Tech) courses and Ph.D. Programs at various IITs, NITs, IISc, GFTIs, and other reputed Universities. Moreover, scores obtained in the GATE exam are also considered by several foreign universities for admission, such as the Nanyang Technological University (Singapore), National University of Singapore (Singapore), Technical University of Munich (Germany), and Aachen University (Germany), etc. GATE score can also be used to apply for jobs in PSUs, Central Government, and GOI Organizations, as well as in Private institutions. The GATE exam score can be used to get various fellowships such as GATE JRF by CSIR.

How to Fill GATE Application Form

1) Visit GOAPS's official site and register.

2) Once registration is completed, candidates need to visit the GATE Login corner.

3) Fill up the application form.

4) Upload the required documents, like photographs, signatures, etc.

5) Make the payment of exam fees as per the categorical requirements.

6) Click on the submit button.

STEP 1: Registration: Candidates register through the GATE online application system (GOAPS) using provided details.
STEP 2: Login and Form Fill-up: Access the GATE portal, fill out the form with personal and academic details, select preferred exam cities and papers.
STEP 3: Upload Scanned Images: Upload scanned photo, signature, and required documents.
STEP 4: Preview Form: Review and verify all entered details.
STEP 5: Payment: Pay application fees online via net banking/debit card/credit card, varying by category.

Will There be Any Changes Made in the GATE 2025 Syllabus?

Generally, the GATE Syllabus remains the same every year. If any changes are to be made in the syllabus, the authorities inform students in advance. Here are some recent changes:

1) In GATE 2024, IISc, Bangalore introduced a new paper on Data Science and AI Engineering.

2) In 2022, subjects such as ES (Environmental Science and Engineering) and XH (Humanities and Social Sciences) were added to the GATE syllabus.

3) In 2021, NM (Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering) and GE (Geomatics Engineering) were added to the syllabus.

It's essential for students to stay updated with any changes in the syllabus announced by the authorities.
